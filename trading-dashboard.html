Overview

This dashboard provides an interactive trading and market analytics view for US equities, designed to support daily decision-making for a Sales & Trading desk.

The objective is to analyze price action, volatility, volume, and relative performance using historical and intraday market data, allowing traders to quickly identify trends, breakouts, and high-impact movements across multiple stocks.

Built in Tableau using SQL-processed data from a structured data warehouse. Focuses on historical and intraday market behavior only — does not include forecasting or valuation modeling.

Market & Price Data

Equity market data stored in a SQL Server data warehouse, structured using a fact-and-dimension model. Data was queried, cleaned, and modeled using SQL views, then connected directly to Tableau. Two datasets are used due to different data granularities (daily vs intraday), handled via Tableau data blending.

  • Historical daily stock prices — Open, High, Low, Close, Adjusted Close, Volume
  • Intraday stock attributes — Last Bid, Market Cap, Beta, Open, High, Low, Volume
  • Reference data — Ticker, Company, Industry, Exchange

Dashboard Components

Provides a comprehensive view of market behavior, volatility, and relative stock performance. Interactive parameters, filters, and actions allow users to drill from a market-wide overview into a single-ticker focus.

  • Candlestick Chart — Dynamic date selection to analyze price action
  • Bollinger Bands — Adjustable moving average and volatility parameters to identify breakouts
  • Volume Analysis — Traded volume including previous volume highs to confirm price strength
  • Relative Growth Chart — Normalized performance comparison across multiple stocks
  • Total Growth Heat Table — Quickly identify outperformers and underperformers
  • Intraday BANS Metrics — Key real-time indicators: beta, market cap, latest bid, daily highs/lows
  • Industry & Ticker Treemap — Market weight and performance assessment by sector
  • Dynamic News Feed — Responsive to selected ticker for contextual market insights

Business Value

This dashboard demonstrates the ability to design and build production-grade trading analytics tools from raw SQL data through to interactive visualization — a workflow relevant to trading desks, equity research teams, and investment management operations.

The combination of SQL data engineering and Tableau front-end shows end-to-end ownership of the analytics stack, from data modeling to business-ready output.
