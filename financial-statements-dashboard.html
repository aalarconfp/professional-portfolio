Power BI · Live Dashboard Financiero Terpel

Overview

This dashboard provides a structured analysis of Terpel's financial performance over the past four years. The project was initiated because the stock appeared undervalued in recent years, requiring a rigorous review of historical trends and financial indicators to support an informed investment decision.

The data pipeline is fully automated and reusable — any Colombian company registered with Supersociedades can be loaded without rebuilding the model, reducing analysis time from days to hours.

Dashboard interface is in Spanish, reflecting the Colombian company context. Historical data only; forward projections require integration with a financial model.

Data & Methodology

Data sourced from the Superintendencia de Sociedades (Supersociedades), Colombia's official corporate financial reporting authority. Raw data was extracted, cleaned, and structured in Excel using Power Query, then modeled and visualized in Power BI.

  • Balance Sheet — 4-year historical view of assets, liabilities, and equity
  • Profit & Loss Statement — Revenue, EBITDA, and net income evolution
Official Source ↗ Supersociedades — SIIS Massive Reports

Dashboard Components

Complete view of financial evolution, profitability, and capital structure — suitable for annual or monthly financial health reviews.

  • Financial Position Summary — Yearly indicators across balance sheet and income statement
  • Horizontal Analysis — Year-over-year variation across all major line items
  • Key Financial Ratios — Profitability, operational efficiency, liquidity, and solvency
  • Cash Flow Breakdown — Operating, investing, and financing activities
  • Free Cash Flow (FCF) — Net cash available to equity holders and lenders

Can be converted from annual to monthly or quarterly view without structural changes to the model.

Business Value

This dashboard directly supports investment analysis, credit assessment, and management reporting — the three primary use cases for structured financial statement analysis. For a CFO or finance team, it replaces manual Excel-based reporting with an interactive, always-current view of company health.
