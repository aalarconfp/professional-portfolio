Overview

This dashboard was designed to provide a comprehensive view of personal finances, combining real accounting data, budgeting targets, and execution tracking in a single interface.

The goal is to improve personal financial decision-making through clear, visual, and comparative indicators — tracking whether income, spending, and savings are aligned with defined goals month by month.

Data & Methodology

Accounting data was initially recorded using the Money Pro app to track income, expenses, and investment activity. It was then exported and structured in Excel, serving as the direct data source for Power BI via dynamic integration.

  • Income — All inflows by source and category
  • Expenses — Categorized spending across fixed and variable costs
  • Savings / Investments — Asset purchases, sales, and net position changes
Data Source ↗ Money Pro App

Budget Methodology — 50/30/20 Rule

The budget was built in Excel based on the client's personal income and savings goals, following the 50/30/20 framework as a baseline allocation model:

50%
Basic Expenses
30%
Personal / Discretionary
20%
Savings & Investments

Allocations are adjusted to the client's actual income structure and goals — the 50/30/20 rule serves as a starting framework, not a rigid constraint.

Dashboard Components

Compares targets vs actual performance, displaying monthly, cumulative (YTD), and annual KPIs across all financial categories.

  • Comparative Charts — Income, expenses, and savings vs budget targets
  • Interactive Buttons — Toggle between Income Target %, Budget %, and Net Worth Target %
  • Category Breakdown — Detailed view by month and YTD performance
  • Annual Summaries — By category, counterparty, and financial position
  • Trend Charts — Income, expense, and savings evolution over time

Business Value

This dashboard gives individuals and financial advisors a structured, data-driven view of personal financial health — replacing manual spreadsheet reviews with an interactive tool that flags budget deviations and tracks net worth progression in real time.

Built as a reusable template adaptable to any individual's income structure, expense categories, and savings objectives.
