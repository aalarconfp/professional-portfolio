Power BI · In Construction Real Estate Dashboard

Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes the performance of a real estate portfolio, offering insights into property conditions, financial efficiency, and overall investment health.

The dataset was extracted, structured, and modeled in Excel, then imported into Power BI for advanced visualization and analysis.

Data Fields

Property-level data covers key operational and financial attributes across the full portfolio:

Property location Price Bedrooms Floors Waterfront Condition yr_built yr_renovated Monthly rent Vacancy rate Operating expenses Maintenance cost (yearly) Property taxes (% of value) Insurance (yearly) Capital expenditures (yearly) Property management fee (%) Rent Loan-to-Value Interest rate Amortization years Appreciation rate

Dashboard Sections

  • Portfolio Overview — Property conditions, renovation status, waterfront classification, bedroom and floor distribution, and breakdown by year built
  • Geographical Insights — Interactive maps showing property locations by condition and floor count across U.S. states
  • Financial Metrics — Key ratios and KPIs across four categories: Revenue & Income, Expense & Efficiency, Profitability, and Valuation & Return

Business Value

This dashboard gives real estate investors and property managers a single-view command center for portfolio health — covering everything from occupancy and operating costs to cap rates and return on investment. It replaces fragmented Excel reports with an interactive, filterable view that supports faster, better-informed acquisition and divestment decisions.

Designed for clarity and intuitive financial insight. Built as a reusable template adaptable to any property portfolio dataset.
